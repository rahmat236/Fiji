Slang

Fiji is a multi-race region that also has multi languages. There are many words that are written differently as they pronounce. 'vinaka' pronounced veenaka means thank you. Yes is 'io' (ee or) and no is 'seg a' (senga) the letter g is pronounced 'ng', so Sigatoka is pronounced Singatoka. The letter d is also pronounced with an n before it, so Nadi is spoken as Nandi. You pronounce an m before the letter b, so Toberau is Tomberau, and a c is pronounced 'th' Mamanuca Islands is pronounced Mamanutha Islands. There are slang words that are used most for young people. Kvt is a Slang name for a person with a Fijian nationality, it came from kai-viti which means Fijian in Fijian. Fijindian is the slang for fijians that are Indian, not kaiviti but still Fijian. Choke is a Fijian Slang meaning asking for money. Boso Fijian slang for a boss. Kidizo is the name of a sexy girl who is Fijian
